Her laughter could mend broken days, and her kindness flowed like the river that fed the village fields. Though the world around her was small, her spirit wandered far, dancing in dreams of invention, light, and learning.
Zuri knew—deep in her glowing heart—that one day, her name would echo beyond the hills, a song of hope for others chasing light in quiet corners.
